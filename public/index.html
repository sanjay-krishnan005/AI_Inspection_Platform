Platform Features

field intelligence

Live BIM Integration

Every field capture — voice note, photo, observation — maps directly to its BIM element in real time. Issues, progress and risk flags live inside your digital twin, not a separate app.

Voice capture

Hands-free AI voice recording. Engineers speak naturally — the AI transcribes, classifies and logs the issue with GPS tagging automatically.

Predictive risk detection

AI compares planned vs actual BIM progress and flags schedule risks before they become delays — giving project managers time to act.

Automated reports

AI-generated daily site reports delivered automatically — reducing reporting lag from 72 hours to under 4, with zero manual effort from engineers.
